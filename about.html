About NEXORA

Nova Nexus Hub

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

"Where Innovation Meets Excellence - Empowering Tomorrow's Tech Leaders"

Nova Nexus Hub is the premier innovation and technology center of the ECE Department at Kings Engineering College. We are committed to fostering creativity, technical excellence, and problem-solving skills among students. Through NEXORA, we aim to provide a platform for brilliant minds to showcase their talents and develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

Innovation Challenge

NEXORA is an exciting innovation event where participants compete in Paper Presentation, Project Expo, and Line Follower challenges. This event brings together the brightest minds from our college to showcase their talents.

Collaborative Learning

Work as a Team. Collaborate, brainstorm, and build innovative solutions. Network with fellow students and learn from each other's expertise.

Recognition & Growth

Top 3 teams will receive exciting prizes and recognition. All participants will receive certificates of participation. Gain valuable experience and showcase your skills to faculty and industry.

Three Main Events

Participate in Paper Presentation, Project Expo, or Line Follower events. Each event offers unique challenges and opportunities to showcase your technical skills and creativity.

Learn & Build

Access mentorship, resources, and guidance throughout the event. Learn new technologies, improve your problem-solving skills, and add impressive projects to your portfolio.
