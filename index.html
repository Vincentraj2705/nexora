Paper Presentation

Paper Presentation is a technical event where participants present their innovative ideas, research findings, or project solutions in front of an audience using slides or visuals. It helps improve technical knowledge, communication skills, and confidence while sharing concepts with experts and peers. Participants are evaluated based on originality, clarity, and presentation skills.

Project Expo

Project Expo is an interactive technical event where participants display their innovative projects, working models, and prototypes to solve real-world challenges. It provides a platform to demonstrate practical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities while engaging directly with judges and audiences.

Know More

Line Follower

Line Follower is a robotics-based technical event where participants design and program an autonomous robot to follow a marked path or line on the floor. It tests skills in sensors, programming, and control systems, focusing on speed, accuracy, and efficiency in navigation.
