Register

Project Expo

Bring your innovative projects to life! Showcase working models, demonstrate practical solutions, and present your technical expertise through live project demonstrations.


Rules & Guidelines (Read Carefully)

  • Participants should register only through the website.
  • A Registration fee of ₹120 has to be paid after submitting the form, Pay using the appearing Pop-up.
  • Payment must be done through the website only.
  • Once the payment is successful and verified then only your registration will also be confirmed.
  • No refund will be provided.
  • Food is also included.
  • Participants can register individually or in teams (maximum 2 members).
  • Projects must be original, innovative, and practically implementable.
  • Working models or live demonstrations are mandatory.
  • Each team will get 5–10 minutes to explain and demo the project.
  • One member must clearly explain the problem, solution, and real-world impact.
  • Use of ready-made or copied projects is strictly prohibited.
  • Posters/charts/PPTs may be used only as support to the demo.
  • Teams must bring their own materials, components, and power requirements.
  • Judges will evaluate based on innovation, usefulness, technical knowledge, and presentation.
  • Bonus points for cost-effective and sustainable solutions.
  • Exceeding time limit or rule violation may lead to penalty.
  • Judges' decision will be final.
  • All participants will receive certificates
